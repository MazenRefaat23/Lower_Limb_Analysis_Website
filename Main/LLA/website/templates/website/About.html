Our Story

Just over a year ago we students at "Faculty of Engineering Ain-Shams university" major of computer engineering, became fascinated by the idea of discovering the world through someone else’s eyes. What if we could see through the eyes of disabled people nowadays and focus on the barriers they face in their life ? or see the life of a passionate athlete who can’t follow his dreams because of his lower limb ? it may sound pathetic. but we wanted to make the dream come true and make the closest the closest thing to natural lower limb. while there are many ways to discover new places, events and even feeling. We realized that there is no better way to do this than using the most advanced technologies of the machine learning and IOT modules. We present this with passion to enjoy .

Dr Gamal Abdel Shafy Ebrahim

Dr Gamal A. Ebrahim received his B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in 1994 and 2000 respectively from the Faculty of Engineering, Ain Sham University, Cairo, Egypt. Additionally, He received his Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering in 2004 from University of Miami, Coral Gables, FL, USA. He has been a faculty member at the Computer and Systems Engineering Department, Faculty of Engineering, Ain Shams University since Oct. 2005. He was a post-doctoral associate at University of Miami, Coral Gables, FL, USA from Mar. 2007 until Jan. 2009. His major research interests include intelligent systems, e-learning systems, software engineering, data mining, VoIP SPAM identification, computer communication networks, cloud computing, big-data analytics, network security, and software-defined networks.

Dr Mohamed Ibrahim Mohamed Hassan Awad

Dr Mohamed I. Awad an Associate Professor in Mechatronics and Mechanical Engineering at Ain Shams University working in mechatronic systems design. My current research interests focus on interdisciplinary research such as: smart robotic prosthetic leg, biomechatronic, mechanisms design, automation, robotics, precision farming, smart agricultural and mechanization, autonomous systems design, and smart manufacturing.
